NBA Team Height Distribution Analysis (2001-2024)

Exploring the evolution of roster height patterns
How to use:
  • Tap or hover over any team point on the left chart to see its height distribution
  • Search for a specific team by typing its ID (e.g., 2016GSW for 2016 Golden State Warriors)
  • Points are colored by season - newer seasons appear in lighter colors
  • On iPad: Tap directly on points to select them - the selected team will be highlighted with a red circle
